Why Learn A Second Language?

Think of the people you know. How many of them speak Japanese? The answer is probably zero. Learning Japanese will give you a notable feature: you will achieve something that only a few people had the courage to carry out! People will look at you in a more respectful way: you carried out something tremendously impressive and positively enviable Learning Japanese will positively affect your self-esteem: if you can learn it, you can do everything. The sky is the limit! Specialists have also maintained that learning a foreign language is a good way to stimulate the brain and avoid degeneative diseases like Alzheimer. The harder, the better, and Japanese is hard, which makes it the perfect language to learn.
